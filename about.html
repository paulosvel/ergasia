About City College Sustainability

At City College, sustainability is not just a buzzword — it's a responsibility. We're committed to transforming our campus into a model of environmental stewardship, innovation, and education.

🌱 Our Mission

To lead by example in promoting sustainable practices, reducing environmental impact, and empowering students, faculty, and staff to contribute to a healthier planet.

🌍 Our Vision

A future where education and sustainability go hand-in-hand. We envision a campus that operates with zero waste, 100% clean energy, and a community that champions ecological thinking in every discipline.

♻️ What We Do

  • Organize tree-planting and recycling campaigns
  • Promote bike-sharing and public transport use
  • Reduce single-use plastics on campus
  • Run sustainability workshops, talks, and competitions
  • Collaborate with local organizations for greater impact

🌟 Student Involvement

Sustainability at City College is a student-driven movement. Whether you’re planting a tree, volunteering at an event, or designing a greener building for your final project — your actions matter.

📈 Our Progress So Far

  • 500+ trees planted
  • 30% energy sourced from solar panels
  • 90% campus-wide recycling rate
  • Plastic-free cafeteria since 2023

Join us as we continue building a sustainable future — one step at a time.
