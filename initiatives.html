Our Sustainability Initiatives

City College is proud to lead the way in environmental responsibility. Here are some of the key initiatives that are transforming our campus and community.

🌳 Tree Planting Program

Over 500 trees planted on campus, improving air quality and biodiversity.

☀️ Solar Panel Expansion

30% of our energy comes from on-campus solar installations.

♻️ Zero Waste Challenge

Promoting reusable containers and eliminating single-use plastics.

🚲 Green Transport

Bike sharing stations and incentives for public transit users.

🍃 Sustainable Cafeteria

Locally sourced ingredients, no plastic cutlery, and composting bins available.

📚 Eco Education

Courses, workshops, and seminars focused on climate awareness and green innovation.
